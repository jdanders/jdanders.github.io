DROPCOUNT

A neon-infused puzzle game of chains and strategy.

How to Play

  • Tap a column to drop the current disc.
  • Match numbers: A disc breaks when the number of contiguous discs in its row OR column equals its number.
  • Solid Discs: Gray discs crack when an adjacent disc breaks. They reveal a number after 2 cracks.
  • Chain Reactions: Breaking discs can trigger more breaks for massive bonus points!
  • New Rows: A new row appears from the bottom every 30 drops. Don't let the grid overflow!

Inspired by the original Drop7. Built with Neon Aesthetics.
